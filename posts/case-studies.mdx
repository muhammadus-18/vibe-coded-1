# Writing great case studies

Case studies are the core of a portfolio. Make them skimmable and concrete.

## The structure

### 1) Context

Who is this for? What problem are we solving?

### 2) Constraints

What made it hard? Timeline, platform, technical trade-offs, team size.

### 3) Approach

Show your thinking. Mention alternatives you rejected.

## What to measure

- Performance (LCP, TTFB)
- Accessibility (WCAG AA)
- Business outcomes (conversion, engagement)
