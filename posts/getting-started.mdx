# Getting started

This template is built for **publishing-worthy portfolios**: strong typography, disciplined motion, and content that’s easy to maintain.

## What you can customize

- Site identity lives in `src/lib/site.js`
- Projects live in `src/lib/projects.js`
- Blog posts live in `posts/*.mdx`

## Content philosophy

Write case studies like a story:

1. The problem and constraints
2. The approach (and trade-offs)
3. The outcomes (with metrics if possible)
