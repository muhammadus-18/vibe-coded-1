# Motion system

Motion should **support comprehension**, not compete with it.

## Reveals

Anything with `data-reveal` will animate in when it enters the viewport.

### Reduced motion

If the user has `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`, animations are skipped.

## Micro-interactions

Prefer subtle “lift” and glow over large transforms.
